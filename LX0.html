Lex Luthor

Man of Tomorrow

Genius. Visionary. Ruthless. Lex Luthor believes the future belongs to humanity—free from alien influence.

Philosophy

Lex Luthor sees Superman not as a savior, but as a symbol of human dependency.

  • 🧠 Humanity must evolve through intellect, not alien gifts.
  • ⚖️ Power must be earned, not inherited.
  • 🌍 Earth should be ruled by humans, for humans.

Tech Arsenal

  • 🛰️ Orbital defense satellites with kryptonite payloads
  • 🤖 AI-powered surveillance and combat drones
  • 🔬 Bio-enhancement labs for human augmentation

🕰️ Lex Luthor's Rise to Power

  • 2023: LexCorp unveils orbital defense tech.
  • 2024: Gains political influence during alien crises.
  • 2025: Declares Superman a threat to human sovereignty.
  • 2025: Launches Project Prometheus.
  • 2025: Publicly challenges Superman’s authority.
