Color Wheel & Mixing Guide

A simple color wheel reminder and how to mix some favorite rainbow shades.

Red: A primary color—cannot be mixed from others.
Blue: A primary color—cool and calming.
Yellow: A primary color—bright and sunny.
Green: Mix blue + yellow.
Purple: Mix red + blue.
Orange: Mix red + yellow.
Pink: Mix red + white.
White: Light, space, and softness—used to lighten any color.
