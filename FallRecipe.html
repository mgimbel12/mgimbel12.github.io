🍁 Fall Recipes

Pumpkin Pie: Pumpkin purée, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, eggs, and sweet cream baked in a flaky crust.

Apple Crisp: Warm apples, brown sugar, oats, and butter baked until golden.

Butternut Squash Soup: Roasted squash blended with cream, sage, and a hint of maple.

🇺🇸 History of America’s Favorite Foods

Cornbread: Rooted in Native American maize traditions, later adopted by early settlers.

Apple Pie: Became a symbol of American identity in the 1800s.

Thanksgiving Foods: Turkey, squash, and cranberries reflect Indigenous agricultural knowledge.

🍨 Ice Cream Recipes

Vanilla Bean: Cream, sugar, vanilla bean, churned until silky.

Strawberry Swirl: Fresh berries folded into sweet cream.

Chocolate Fudge: Cocoa, cream, and melted chocolate for richness.

🏝️ Island Favorite Desserts

Haupia (Hawaii): Coconut milk pudding with a soft, silky texture.

Guava Cake: Light sponge cake topped with guava glaze.

Banana Lumpia (Philippines): Sweet bananas wrapped and fried until golden.
