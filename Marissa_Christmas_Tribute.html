🥧 Chicken Pot Pie – BYU-Idaho Style

Ingredients: Chicken, carrots, peas, celery, onion, butter, flour, broth, milk, pie crusts, mashed potatoes.

Twist: Add a layer of creamy mashed potatoes between filling and crust. Sprinkle parmesan and breadcrumbs for a golden finish.

Memory: Inspired by cozy weekends and pot pie dinners at BYU-Idaho—comfort food with a spiritual hug.

🍪 Gingerbread Scripture Scrolls

Ingredients: Warm milk, flour, cinnamon, ginger, brown sugar, cream cheese frosting.

Concept: Roll dough into scrolls, bake, and top with edible scripture verses like Isaiah 9:6 or Luke 2:11.

Visual: Serve with golden ribbon and glowing icing swirls. Perfect for gifting or devotionals.

👼 Floating Angel Illustrations with Glowing Halos

Ingredients: Sugar cookie dough, gelatin wings or rice paper, lemon icing, edible gold dust.

Art Concept: Place cookies on cloud-shaped meringues. Surround with fairy lights for a floating glow effect.

Scripture: “Glory to God in the highest” – Luke 2:14

Made with love, light, and laughter ✨
