Sam Jones III

Early Life

Samuel L. Jones III (born April 29, 1983) is an American actor best known for his role as Pete Ross in Smallville.

Career

Early Career Highlights

Jones portrayed Pete Ross in the first three seasons of Smallville, based on Superman’s early years. He returned briefly in Season 7’s episode "Hero."

Pete Ross & Friends

Allison Mack and Pete Ross were close characters on Smallville. Their interactions added emotional depth to the show's storyline.
