Welcome to the Mets vs Dodgers Game

Get ready for an electrifying matchup between two legendary teams. Whether you're a die-hard fan or new to the sport, there's something magical about baseball under the stadium lights.

Meet the Teams

New York Mets

The Mets, founded in 1962, are known for their passionate fanbase and iconic moments in baseball history. Based in Queens, New York, they play at Citi Field and are part of the National League East Division.

Los Angeles Dodgers

The Dodgers, originally from Brooklyn, moved to Los Angeles in 1958. With multiple World Series titles and a legacy of legendary players, they are one of the most successful franchises in MLB history.
