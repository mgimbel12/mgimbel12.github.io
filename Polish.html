Nails & Easter Egg Hunt — Step by Step

💅 Step by Step: Easter Nails

  1. Clean: Wash hands and remove old polish.
  2. Shape: File nails into your favorite shape (round, square, almond).
  3. Prep: Push back cuticles gently and buff the surface lightly.
  4. Base Coat: Apply a thin base coat and let it dry.
  5. Color: Paint 1–2 thin coats of pastel polish (pink, lilac, mint, sky blue).
  6. Design: Add tiny flowers, dots, or mini Easter eggs if you want.
  7. Top Coat: Seal with a glossy top coat for shine and protection.

🥚 Step by Step: Easter Egg Hunt

  1. Goal: Click eggs to “search” them.
  2. Hidden: Some eggs are empty, some have candy, one is a golden egg.
  3. Try: Click each egg to reveal what’s inside.
  4. Count: Watch how many eggs you’ve found.
  5. Win: Find the golden egg to finish the hunt.
