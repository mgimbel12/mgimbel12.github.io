Easter Nail Polish Steps

Fill in each step with your own colors, designs, and little rituals for Easter‑time nails.

🐣 Worksheet • Tutorial • Ceremony for your hands
1
PREP & CLEAN

Prep the canvas (hands + nails)

Fill in: how do you want to wash, shape, and prepare your nails before the Easter colors go on?

Example: “Wash hands, trim, file into soft oval, push back cuticles, buff lightly.”

2
BASE COAT

Protect & smooth (base layer)

Fill in: what base coat or clear polish will you use, and what does it help you with?

Example: “One thin layer of strengthening base coat, let it dry fully.”

3
EASTER COLORS

Choose your palette (pastels or brights)

Fill in: which colors are you using on each nail? You can list fingers or create a pattern.

Example: “Alternate pink and lilac, 2 thin coats, let each coat dry.”

4
DESIGNS & DETAILS

Add Easter art (optional)

Fill in: dots, stripes, tiny eggs, bunnies, crosses, flowers—what details do you want to add?

Example: “White dots on all nails, tiny flowers on ring fingers.”

5
TOP COAT

Seal the design (shine + protection)

Fill in: what top coat will you use, and how will you apply it to protect your art?

Example: “One glossy top coat, cap the free edge of each nail.”

6
DRY & CELEBRATE

Let them set (and enjoy)

Fill in: how long will you let them dry, and what Easter moment are you getting ready for?

Example: “Dry 30 minutes while I plan my Easter outfit.”
