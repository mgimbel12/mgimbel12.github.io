Understanding the Purpose of Cooking

Cooking is more than preparing foodâ€”it's about nourishment, creativity, and safety. Whether you're cooking for yourself or others, mastering the basics is key to success.

Step 1: Wash Your Hands

Wash your hands thoroughly for at least 20 seconds, scrubbing between fingers and under nails. Singing the alphabet song is a fun way to time it!

Step 2: Prepare Yourself

Before handling food, put on a hair net and apron to maintain hygiene and protect your clothes.

Step 3: Wear Gloves

Use disposable gloves to prevent contamination. Make sure they fit snugly and are changed regularly.

Step 4: Safe Cutting Techniques

Always use a clean cutting board and a sharp knife. Keep fingers tucked and cut away from your body.
