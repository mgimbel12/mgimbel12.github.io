🍕 Homemade pizza recipe

Ingredients

A simple, cozy homemade pizza—because some of the best stories are told over slices.

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • Tomato sauce, mozzarella, and your favorite toppings

Steps

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit until foamy.
  2. Stir in flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise in a warm place until doubled.
  4. Stretch onto a pan, add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  5. Bake at 475°F (245°C) for 10–14 minutes, until golden.

Serve, share, and let the conversation wander—this page is the backdrop, the pizza is the centerpiece.
