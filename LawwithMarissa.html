Hair, Cosmos & Logic Basics

Basic steps of hair treatments, plus style types and fashion statements—because briefs and blowouts can coexist.

Basic Hair Treatment Steps
  • Cleanse: gentle shampoo, massage scalp.
  • Condition: mid-lengths to ends, detangle slowly.
  • Treat: mask, oil, or leave-in for your hair type.
  • Protect: heat protectant before styling.
  • Finish: light hold, shine, or texture as needed.
Hair Style Types & Statements
  • Ponytail: “I’m efficient and focused.”
  • Curls defined: “I’m bringing softness and power.”
  • Sleek bun: “I’m in courtroom mode.”
  • Braids: “I remember where I come from.”
