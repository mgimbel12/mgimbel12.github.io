When the clouds roll in
Feelings & signs

Naming your feelings is like turning on a little lantern in the dark.

Tiny bits of magic
Calming actions
Slow belly breaths (in 4, out 4) Hold a favorite toy Look for 5 things I like in the room Ask for a hug

You don’t have to fix everything—just choose one kind, gentle action for right now.

Safe places in my world
Comfort zones

A safe place can be a room, a chair, a corner, or anywhere you feel more okay being yourself.

My safety team
Trusted people

Let these people know they are on your “safety team” so they understand how important they are.

If it feels like an emergency
Immediate safety

If you ever feel like you might hurt yourself or someone else, tell a trusted adult right away and treat it like an emergency.
