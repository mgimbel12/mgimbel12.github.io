World Holidays Explorer

This page introduces holidays from different regions of the world, including the United States, Nevada, California, Utah, Mexico, Japan, China, and Peru. You can select a region and search by name to explore how people celebrate important days in many places.

Choose your holidays

You can select a region or country from the list and you can type a word to search for a specific holiday. The list on the right will update automatically and will show only the holidays that match your choices.

This is a simple HTML, CSS, and JavaScript project that demonstrates how to display holidays from different regions in a clear and friendly way.
