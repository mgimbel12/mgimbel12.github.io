Safety • Respect

Police Officer

Protecting neighborhoods and keeping the peace.

Police officers respond to emergencies, guide traffic, and help keep communities safe. They build trust by listening, staying calm, and treating people fairly—on the street, at schools, and during community events.
🚒
Courage • Teamwork

Firefighter

Answering the call when seconds matter.

Firefighters put out fires, rescue people, and teach families about safety plans. Their gear is heavy, their training is intense, and their teamwork helps them stay brave and focused when others are in danger.
📣
Leadership • School Spirit

Cheer Captain

Leading with kindness, confidence, and modesty.

A modest cheer captain focuses on encouragement, teamwork, and positive energy. Their routines and uniforms stay respectful while they cheer on classmates, welcome younger students, and make sure everyone feels included on the team.
