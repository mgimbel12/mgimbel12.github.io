Tales of Tales: Safety Plan

Lex Luthor Verse – when the mastermind learns to protect, not destroy.
In towers of steel and neon glare,
I drafted blueprints for despair.
But every scheme, each ruthless chart,
Collided with a fragile heart.

So now my plans are redrawn, clean—
Not world’s end, but a safer scene.
A map for storms inside the mind,
Where every step is: breathe, be kind.

Safety Plan – Issue #1

Step 1: Notice the signal.
Racing thoughts, tight chest, wanting to hide from everyone.
Step 2: Ground in the present.
Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, 1 you can taste.
Step 3: Call your allies.
Trusted friends, family, or a professional you can talk to about what you’re feeling.
Step 4: Choose one safe action.
Drink water, step outside, journal, listen to calming music, or rest in a quiet space.
This is a fictional universe. For real-life crises, talk to someone you trust or a qualified professional.
