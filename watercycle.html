Water Cycle

  • Evaporation
  • Condensation
  • Precipitation
  • Runoff
  • Transpiration
  • Sublimation

The sun heats water in rivers, lakes, and oceans, causing it to evaporate into the air as water vapor.

Condensation

Water vapor in the air changes into liquid water, forming clouds, fog, or dew.

Precipitation

Water falls from clouds as rain, snow, sleet, or hail.

Runoff

Water that falls as precipitation flows over the land and into rivers, lakes, and oceans.

Transpiration

Plants and trees lose water to the atmosphere through their leaves.

Sublimation

Snow and ice evaporate into water vapor.
