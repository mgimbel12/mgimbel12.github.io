Workforce • Employment

Access & Opportunity

Workforce access means that every person has a fair chance to find work, learn new skills, and grow in a safe and respectful environment.

  • Everyone deserves clear information about job openings.
  • Applications should be simple, respectful, and accessible.
  • Training and support help people succeed, not just “get hired.”
  • Workplaces should welcome different abilities, backgrounds, and strengths.
Helpers

Workforce Helpers & Questions

Use these full‑sentence prompts to guide your thinking:

  • I can ask, “What support do I need to do this job well?”
  • I can say, “Please explain the steps in simple language.”
  • I can write, “Here are the skills I already have and want to grow.”
  • I can remember, “My value is not measured only by my job title.”
Guidelines

Full‑Sentence Guidelines for Learning

  • I will use clear, kind language when I talk about myself and others.
  • I will ask questions when I do not understand a word or idea.
  • I will give myself time to practice new skills without shame.
  • I will remember that learning is a lifelong process, not a race.
Vocabulary Helpers

Workforce & Digital Age Vocabulary

  • Access: The ability to reach, use, or benefit from something.
  • Employment: A working relationship where a person is paid for their time and skills.
  • Digital Age: A time when computers, phones, and the internet shape how we live and work.
  • Robotics: The design and use of machines that can move and act with some level of control.
  • Concept Art: Drawings or images that explore ideas before they are built or filmed.

Digital Age

Robotics Ideas & Art Concepts

Robotics in the digital age can be gentle, helpful, and creative. Robots can assist with lifting heavy objects, exploring dangerous places, or helping people communicate.

  • A small robot that delivers books or supplies in a library.
  • A drawing robot that paints patterns based on music.
  • A classroom robot that helps students practice vocabulary and math.
  • A gentle robot arm that helps with art projects or science experiments.

Art concepts can show robots and humans working together with respect, kindness, and curiosity.
