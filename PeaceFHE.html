🕊️ Family Home Evening: Peace Through Christ

Tonight we gather to reflect on the Savior’s power to calm our hearts and guide our lives.

“I am held in Christ’s peace. His love surrounds me.”

🎶 Opening Song

“Where Can I Turn for Peace?” – Invite everyone to sing or listen quietly.

📖 Scripture

John 14:27 — “Peace I leave with you, my peace I give unto you: not as the world giveth…”

🧠 Discussion Prompt

  • What does peace feel like to you?
  • When have you felt the Holy Ghost bring comfort?
  • How can we invite Christ’s peace into our home this week?

🎥 Family Home Evening Video: Fruit of the Spirit

Watch this gentle guide to Christlike attributes. Pause at “Peace” (around 2:16) to reflect together.

🎨 Activity: Peace Garden

Each person draws a garden filled with symbols of peace—flowers, light, water, doves. Share what each symbol means to you.

📝 Journal Moment

Write a short prayer or gratitude note to the Savior. What are you thankful for today?

🌟 Closing Affirmation

“Christ walks with me. I am never alone.”

🙏 Closing Prayer

Invite someone to offer a prayer focused on gratitude, comfort, and peace.

🍪 Optional Treat

Share a calming treat—herbal tea, warm cookies, or fruit—and reflect on the sweetness of Christ’s love.
