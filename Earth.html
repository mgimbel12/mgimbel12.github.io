How to describe the clouds.

Stratus Clouds, "stray" + "tuhs klowdz"

Stratus Clouds:Low-altitude clouds that form flat layers and can make the sky appear gray or overcast.

Altocumulus clouds "al"+"toh"+"kyoo"+"myuh"+"luhs"

Form in the middle troposphere and can indicate atmospheric instability.

cirrus clouds

high-altitude clouds made of ice crystal(s) that look wispy and feathery.

Cumulus Clouds

puffy white clouds with flat bases that are often seen on sunny days.

Nimbostratus clouds

A special rain clouds catergory which combined the three forms cumbo + cirro + stratus.

Cirrocumulus clouds

thin, white, puffy,clouds that appear in a row or patches of sky.
