Santa Cookies

Tools: Mixing Bowls, Baking Sheet, Silicone Mat, Cookie Scoop, Cooling Rack

Ingredients:

  • 15.25 oz red velvet cake mix (1 box)
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup red, white & blue M&Ms
  • ½ cup granulated sugar for coating

Instructions:

  1. Combine cake mix, oil, and eggs in a large bowl or stand mixer. Beat until combined.
  2. Fold in half of the M&Ms.
  3. Chill dough for 60 minutes (do not skip).
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment or silicone mat.
  5. Use cookie scoop to form balls, roll in sugar, and place 2 inches apart on sheet.
  6. Bake for 8–9 minutes. Cookies will look puffy and slightly underdone.
  7. Press remaining M&Ms into tops immediately after baking. Cool on sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack.

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: American
Keyword: Cake Mix Cookies, Red Velvet Cookies
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 9 minutes
Chilling Time: 1 hour
Total Time: 1 hour 14 minutes
Servings: 24 cookies
Calories: 169 kcal
Author: Julie Kotzbach

These easy Red Velvet Cake Mix Cookies are a family favorite, with a festive twist using holiday-themed M&Ms!
