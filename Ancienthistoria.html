Ancient Historia: America

Echoes of the First Peoples

Introduction

Long before modern borders, the lands of the Americas were home to countless nations, stories, sciences, and ceremonies. From the Arctic to the Andes, communities observed the stars, cultivated the soil, and built cities that still whisper through stone.

Major Ancient Civilizations

Olmec

Known for colossal stone heads and early developments in writing, astronomy, and ceremonial centers along the Gulf Coast.

Maya

Masters of calendars, writing, mathematics, and astronomy. Their cities rose like emerald constellations across the rainforest.

Aztec

Builders of Tenochtitlan, floating gardens, marketplaces, and a rich tradition of poetry, philosophy, and ritual.

Inca

Engineers of the Andes: terraced agriculture, vast road networks, and cities like Machu Picchu woven into mountain stone.

Simple Timeline

  • c. 250–900 CEClassic Maya period: cities, astronomy, writing.
