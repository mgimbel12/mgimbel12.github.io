About Tweety

Tweety Bird, known for the iconic line "I tawt I taw a puddy tat!", is a beloved Looney Tunes character. With big eyes and clever quips, Tweety has been outsmarting Sylvester the Cat since 1942. His playful personality and innocent appearance mask a surprisingly witty edge.

🎉 Birthday Tribute

Enjoy this cheerful birthday compilation of Tweety moments, featuring feathered fun and animated joy.

🐦 Other Feathered Friends

  • Woodstock – Snoopy’s loyal pal from Peanuts
  • Zazu – The watchful hornbill from The Lion King
  • Mordecai – The quirky blue jay from Regular Show
  • Big Bird – Sesame Street’s gentle giant

🎈 Fun Fact

Tweety was originally pink! His yellow color came later when animation studios realized pink feathers resembled plucked chicken.
