My Testimony

I share my testimony of the truth of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The truth to be told—you must hold on to the Iron Rod.

Make the days of your life worthy of something and become the one person you can truly love through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

I believe the Holy Bible to be the word of God, and that the Book of Mormon, as long as it is translated correctly, is the word of Heaven’s mouthpiece.

I believe in the Holy Ghost, the gift of receiving, and the belief in Jesus Christ.
