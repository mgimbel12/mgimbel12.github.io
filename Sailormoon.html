Welcome to the Sailor Moon fan page! Dive into the magical story of friendship, adventure, and love.

Sailor Moon (Japanese: 美少女戦士セーラームーン, Hepburn: Bishōjo Senshi Sērā Mūn) is a beloved Japanese manga series created by Naoko Takeuchi. Originally serialized from 1991 to 1997, the story follows Usagi Tsukino as she transforms into Sailor Moon and searches for the "Legendary Silver Crystal" (幻の銀水晶, Maboroshi no Ginsuishō). Along the way, she joins forces with other Sailor Guardians to protect the universe from evil.

Summary adapted from the Wikipedia article on Sailor Moon.

📖 Did You Know?

Although Sailor Moon is Japanese, it's often celebrated worldwide, with strong fan bases in China, the US, and beyond. The manga and anime emphasize themes of hope, courage, and friendship that transcend cultural boundaries.
