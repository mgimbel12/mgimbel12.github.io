📘 Key Terms & Definitions

Variable
A symbol (usually a letter) that represents an unknown value. Example: x, y, n.
Coefficient
The number multiplied by a variable. Example: in 4x, the coefficient is 4.
Constant
A number that does not change. Example: 7, −3, 12.
Expression
A math phrase with numbers, variables, and operations. Example: 3x + 5.
Equation
A statement showing two expressions are equal. Example: 2x + 1 = 7.
Solution
The value that makes an equation true. Example: x = 3.
Linear Equation
An equation whose graph is a straight line. Example: y = mx + b.
Slope (m)
The steepness of a line. Formula: (change in y) ÷ (change in x).
Y‑Intercept (b)
Where the line crosses the y‑axis.
