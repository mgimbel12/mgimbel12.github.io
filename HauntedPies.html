Spellbound Pie Recipes

Classic comfort pies, dressed up for a night in a haunted mansion.

  • Grinning Ghost Apple Pie
    Flaky butter crust • Cinnamon-spiced apples • Haunted caramel drizzle
    Steps:
    1. Toss sliced apples with sugar, cinnamon, and a pinch of nutmeg.
    2. Line a pie dish with chilled butter crust, fill with apples, dot with butter.
    3. Bake at 375°F until bubbling and golden, then drizzle warm caramel in ghostly swirls.
    4. Serve slightly warm—perfect for storytelling in dim candlelight.
    Apple Caramel Haunted Theme
  • Rocky Mountain Chocolate Silk Pie
    Silky chocolate filling • Whipped cream peaks • Denver Broncos game-night favorite
    Steps:
    1. Whisk melted dark chocolate into a smooth custard with eggs, sugar, and cream.
    2. Pour into a baked cookie crust and chill until set and glossy.
    3. Top with whipped cream peaks and a sprinkle of cocoa before kickoff.
    4. Slice small—this one is rich like an overtime win.
    Chocolate Game Night Broncos Colors
  • Salt Lake Berry Jazz Pie
    Mixed berries • Lattice crust • Utah Jazz purple & gold sugar dust
    Steps:
    1. Combine blueberries, raspberries, and blackberries with sugar and lemon zest.
    2. Fill a crust-lined dish and top with a lattice of chilled dough strips.
    3. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle purple and gold sanding sugar.
    4. Bake at 375°F until the filling sings and the crust is crisp.
    Berry Lattice Utah Jazz
  • Midnight Mansion Pumpkin Pie
    Spiced pumpkin custard • Shadowy chocolate crust • Whipped “fog” topping
    Steps:
    1. Stir pumpkin purée with brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves.
    2. Blend in eggs and cream, then pour into a chocolate cookie crust.
    3. Bake at 350°F until just set in the center; cool completely.
    4. Top with softly whipped cream clouds and a dusting of cocoa “fog.”
    Pumpkin Chocolate Crust Spooky

👻Walt Disney’s Haunted Mansion Vibes

Turn your kitchen into a friendly haunted corridor—no doom buggies required.

Atmosphere Checklist

  • Candlelit Corridor
    Dim the lights, use flickering LED candles, and play soft organ music while pies bake. The goal: cozy, not scary—like the ride, where the ghosts are more playful than frightening.
  • Talking Portraits
    Print vintage-style family photos, frame them, and place them near the dessert table. Add handwritten captions like “Beware the pie thief” for a whimsical touch.
  • Ghostly Pie Labels
    Use small cards shaped like tombstones or ghosts to label each pie: “Grinning Ghost Apple,” “Midnight Mansion Pumpkin,” and “Bewitched Berry Jazz.”
