Poem for America

From purple mountain mornings, to cities lit at night, We gather under stripes and stars, in freedom’s gentle light. Voices rise in quiet thanks, for journeys long and wide, For every hand that lifts another, standing side by side. We carry stories, old and new, in hearts both brave and kind, A patchwork hope of many dreams, in one great quilt combined. O beautiful, this living land, where love can plant its seed, And families share a simple day—that’s all the grace we need.

Family Activities to Celebrate

  • Backyard Picnic Under the Stars
    Spread a blankqet, share favorite foods, and take turns naming constellations or making up your own.
  • Gratitude Fireworks (Without the Noise)
    Cut out paper stars and stripes. On each, write something you’re grateful for about your country or community, then hang them as a banner.
  • Family Story Circle
    Invite each person to share a story about a time they felt proud, brave, or helped someone. Record them to create a “Family Freedom Journal.”
  • Neighborhood Kindness Walk
    Take a walk together and look for small ways to serve—picking up litter, leaving kind notes, or waving to neighbors.
  • Sing-Along & Flag Craft
    Make simple flags from paper and crayons, then sing “America the Beautiful” or other favorite songs while you wave them.
