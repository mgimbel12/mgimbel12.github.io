Principal

System Guardian • Vision Anchor • Culture Architect
Earth‑side Command

In this Smallville‑style verse, the Principal is the system guardian — the one who holds the whole town’s school in orbit. They balance instruction, culture, and operations so that every classroom has a clear sky to grow in.

Operational Leader

Counselor & Student Council

Heart of the System • Student Voice • Hero Team
Student‑Side Command

The School Counselor is the heart of the system — the quiet hero who walks the halls with a lantern, seeing both the individual story and the whole map. The Student Council is the hero team: presidents, reps, and organizers who carry student voice into the center of the town’s decisions.

Constellation: Advocacy • Belonging • Student Voice

Advocacy Leader

Stands in the gap for students who feel unseen, challenging barriers and unfair patterns.

Equity & access shield

Mental Health Guide

Holds space for big feelings, crisis moments, and the slow work of healing and coping.

Emotional first‑response

Program Designer

Builds counseling programs with data and care — lessons, groups, and supports that reach many.

Systemic interventions

Bridge Builder

Connects students, families, teachers, and community partners into one circle of support.

Web of relationships

President
