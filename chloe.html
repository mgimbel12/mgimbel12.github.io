🧠 Chloe Sullivan

Chloe Sullivan, played by Allison Mack, is Smallville’s resident journalist and tech genius. Founder of the Torch, she uncovered secrets, supported Clark Kent, and became a key player in the fight against LuthorCorp and other threats.
