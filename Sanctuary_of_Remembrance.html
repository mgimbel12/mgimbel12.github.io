Prayer of Light and Remembrance

Holy Spirit, enter this space with grace.
Let every flicker of light be a memory honored,
every floating star a soul remembered.

Bless those who carry sorrow in silence,
and those who seek healing in the quiet.
May this sanctuary be a place of peace,
where hope rises like dawn and love never fades.

We remember. We reflect. We renew.
In Your presence, we are whole.
