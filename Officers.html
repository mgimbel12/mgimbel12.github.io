Emergency numbers and calling guidance

In the U.S., dial 911 for emergencies. For the Las Vegas area, non-emergency police is 311. Henderson Police non-emergency is 702-267-5000. Use 911 if there is immediate danger to life, health, or property.

  • If in doubt: call 911. Describe the situation, your location, and any immediate risks clearly.
  • Non-emergency: use 311 or your local non-emergency line for noise complaints, past incidents, or general concerns.
  • Stay safe: do not put yourself at risk to gather details; share what you know, then move to safety.

Many countries use a short emergency number like 911 or 112; in the U.S., 911 reaches local emergency services. For Las Vegas, LVMPD lists 311 as non‑emergency. Henderson Police publishes 702‑267‑5000 for non‑emergency.
