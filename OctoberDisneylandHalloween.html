October at the Disneyland Castle

On a cool October night, as shimmering fall leaves drifted across the path leading to the softly illuminated Disneyland Castle, families gathered around a table of Halloween treat recipes—warm caramel-dipped apples, cinnamon-spiced pumpkin bars, and chocolate-swirled ghost cookies—while a meticulously carved Halloween pumpkin, glowing with intricate patterns of stars, vines, and smiling faces, stood as the cheerful centerpiece of the magical autumn celebration.
