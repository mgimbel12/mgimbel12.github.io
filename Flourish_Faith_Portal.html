🍲 Chicken Pot Pie of Belonging

  • 2 cups cooked chicken
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 cup cream of chicken soup
  • 1 pie crust, golden and flaky

Bake at 375°F until bubbly and golden. Serve with a prayer of gratitude.

Food Safety Tip: Internal temp must reach 165°F (74°C).
