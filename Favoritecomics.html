About Lex

A genius, billionaire, and ruthless strategist, Lex Luthor believes the greatest threat to humanity... is Superman. His obsession with control, justice, and power has made him one of the most iconic villains in comic history.

Notable Quotes

  • "Brains, not brawn."
  • "You can't rely on the world to save itself. It needs vision. It needs me."
  • "If there's one thing I admire about Superman, it's that he inspires others. Shame they never think for themselves."
