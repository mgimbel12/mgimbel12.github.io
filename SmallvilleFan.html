Season Overview

Smallville follows Clark Kent through his formative years in the town of Smallville, Kansas. The series blends coming-of-age drama with superhero mythology, focusing on his relationships with Lana, Chloe, Lex, Lois, the Kents, and the wider hero community.

This fan-club sheet organizes the major “ships,” cast details, and thematic importance of each character into a single, printable or shareable document.

Relationship Spotlights

Lexana (Lex & Lana)

A complex relationship where genuine affection collides with power and secrecy. For Lex, Lexana is an attempt at a normal life; for Lana, it marks her transition into the dangerous Luthor world.

Chlois

A fan concept that frames Chloe as a proto-Lois Lane: investigative, sharp, and fearless. It highlights her role as the show’s original reporter archetype.

Chlana (Chloe & Lana)

A friendship that survives secrets, crushes, and shifting loyalties. Chlana showcases how two very different young women learn to trust each other.

Lois & Lex

Their scenes emphasize ideological contrast: Lois’s moral compass versus Lex’s calculated pragmatism, mirroring the conflict between free press and power.

Chlark (Chloe & Clark)

A deep emotional bond built on loyalty and investigative partnership. Chlark anchors Clark’s early hero’s journey, especially before Clois becomes central.

Clois (Clark & Lois)

The iconic endgame romance. Clois evolves from banter and rivalry into a partnership defined by mutual respect and shared purpose.

Chimmy (Chloe & Jimmy)

A warm, grounded relationship that explores how secrets and danger strain everyday love.

Clana (Clark & Lana)

The central early-series romance, representing first love and the pain of hidden truths. Clana shows how secrecy can erode even the strongest feelings.

Oliver & Chloe

A later-series partnership blending tech genius with vigilante leadership. Their bond emphasizes trust, shared mission, and emotional healing.

Ollie (Oliver Queen)

Oliver challenges Clark to think globally and act decisively, expanding the show into a team-based hero universe.

AC Curry & Merria

Arthur Curry (Aquaman) brings oceanic stakes and environmental themes into Smallville. “Merria” can serve as a fan tag for his romantic and royal future.

Importance by Character

Lex Luthor

Lexana, Clex, and his family ties chart his journey from wounded friend to full antagonist, exploring trust, betrayal, and power.

Chloe Sullivan

Through Chlark, Chimmy, Chlana, and Oliver, Chloe becomes a lens on journalism, sacrifice, and unrequited love.

Lana Lang

Clana and Lexana trace her path from small-town innocence to a woman claiming her own agency and boundaries.

Clark Kent

His relationships with Lana, Chloe, Lois, Lex, Oliver, and the Kents shape his moral compass and destiny.

Jonathan Kent

Clark’s moral anchor, modeling integrity, restraint, and sacrificial love within the Kent family.

Lex’s Circle

Lionel, friends, and rivals become mirrors for what Lex could have been, highlighting nature vs. nurture and legacy.
