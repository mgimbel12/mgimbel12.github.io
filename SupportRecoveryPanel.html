Safety & Ground Rules

Everyone deserves to feel physically and emotionally safe. We use calm voices, clear boundaries, and we pause when things feel overwhelming. Real emergencies are handled by trusted authorities and professionals.

  • ✓ Respect: No insults, threats, or manipulation—only honest, calm communication.
  • ✓ Consent: We ask before deep conversations and accept “not now” as an answer.
  • ✓ Support: We encourage therapy, counseling, and crisis lines when needed.

Family, Friends & Advocates

Healthy loyalty means telling the truth, respecting the law, and encouraging loved ones to get the help they need. Advocates listen, document concerns, and connect people with services—rather than hiding problems.

  • ✓ Family: Offers emotional support without covering up harmful behavior.
  • ✓ Friends: Encourage rest, hobbies, and joy while staying honest about safety.
  • ✓ Advocates: Help navigate services, legal options, and mental health resources.
