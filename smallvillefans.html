📚 Series Overview

Debut: October 16, 2001
Finale: May 13, 2011
Created by Alfred Gough and Miles Millar, Smallville chronicles the life of young Clark Kent in Kansas before he becomes Superman.

🚀 Record Breaker

Smallville achieved 8.4 million viewers in its debut, later surpassing Stargate SG-1 as the longest-running North American sci-fi series.
