Nicolas Hoult

From *About a Boy* to *Superman*—a career of transformation

🎭 Biography

Nicolas Hoult, born December 7, 1989, in Berkshire, England, is a versatile actor known for roles in *X-Men*, *Mad Max: Fury Road*, *The Great*, and *Superman* (2025). He began acting at age 3 and rose to fame with *About a Boy* (2002).

🎬 Career Highlights

  • About a Boy (2002)
  • Skins (2007–2008)
  • X-Men: First Class (2011)
  • Mad Max: Fury Road (2015)
  • The Favourite (2018)
  • The Menu (2022)
  • Superman (2025) – as Lex Luthor
