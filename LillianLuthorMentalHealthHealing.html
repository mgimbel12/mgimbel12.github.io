Lillian learns early that her value is tied to image and control. Feelings are dangerous, vulnerability is shameful, and love is conditional. This emotional environment lays the groundwork for depression, anxiety, and rigid perfectionism that never gets named or treated.

Reflection & Healing Possibilities
Lillian is often framed as cold and cruel, but underneath is a woman shaped by pressure, fear, and untreated pain. Her control is a shield, not a cure.
  • Notice how often she chooses control when she is actually afraid.
  • Track the moments where tenderness flickers through the armor.
  • Ask what might have changed if she’d had a safe place to be honest.
