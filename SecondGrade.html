Hero File

ELA • Storylight

Reading, writing, speaking, and listening powers.

  • Read stories and informational texts, naming characters, setting, problem, and solution.
  • Ask and answer questions about key details using who, what, where, when, why, and how.
  • Use phonics to decode long and short vowels, two‑syllable words, and common patterns.
  • Write opinion pieces with reasons, informational paragraphs, and small‑moment narratives.
  • Use sequence words (first, next, then, finally) to organize ideas clearly.
  • Share ideas in discussions and retell stories with important details.

Math • Numberguard

Number sense, operations, and problem‑solving.

  • Understand place value to 1,000 and explain the value of each digit.
  • Add and subtract within 100 using strategies and written methods.
  • Recognize even and odd numbers and simple patterns.
  • Tell time to the nearest 5 minutes and read simple schedules.
  • Count money using coins and bills to solve real‑world problems.
  • Identify and partition shapes into halves, thirds, and fourths.

Science • Wonderfield

Earth, life, and physical science investigations.

  • Observe weather patterns and seasons, recording changes over time.
  • Explore rocks, soil, and water as Earth materials.
  • Describe plant and animal needs, habitats, and simple ecosystems.
  • Study life cycles such as butterflies, frogs, and plants.
  • Investigate motion, pushes and pulls, sound, and light.
  • Use simple experiments: predict, test, observe, and share results.

Social Studies • Heartland

Community, geography, history, and citizenship.

  • Use maps and globes to locate places and follow simple directions.
  • Compare urban, suburban, and rural communities.
  • Recognize national symbols and important American holidays.
  • Read short biographies of influential Americans and create simple timelines.
  • Discuss rules, responsibilities, and ways to solve problems together.
  • Practice classroom citizenship: respect, responsibility, and cooperation.

Hero File

Specials • Creative Core

Art, music, and physical education.

  • Explore color mixing, lines, shapes, and textures in art projects.
  • Create seasonal and cultural artwork that connects to class themes.
  • Keep a steady beat, echo rhythm patterns, and sing simple melodies.
  • Identify common instruments and listen for musical elements.
  • Practice balance, coordination, and movement in PE activities.
  • Show teamwork, sportsmanship, and safe play during games.

Hero File

SEL • Kindness Core

Feelings, friendships, and growth mindset.

  • Identify and name feelings in self and others.
  • Practice calm‑down strategies and simple problem‑solving steps.
  • Use kind words, listening ears, and sharing in friendships.
  • Learn that mistakes help the brain grow (growth mindset).
  • Set small goals and celebrate effort and progress.
  • Contribute to a classroom that feels safe, welcoming, and fair.
