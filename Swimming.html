🛟 Unit 1 — Water Safety & Aquatic Foundations

Safety · Comfort · Confidence
Topics & skills view

Topics

Skills

By the end of this course, students will be able to:
  • Demonstrate safe behavior in and around pools and open water.
  • Swim all major strokes—freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and introductory butterfly—with efficient technique.
  • Use breathing patterns and pacing to swim multiple laps with endurance.
  • Perform basic lifesaving assists and respond appropriately to aquatic emergencies within their training level.
  • Explain how swimming supports lifelong fitness, stress relief, and community engagement.
