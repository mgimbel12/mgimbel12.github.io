Bright Orange Smoothie

Zesty, energizing, and alive with color. Blend, sip, and glow.

Vitamin C 5 min Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh oranges, peeled
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 small carrot (optional, for color)
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 1 tbsp honey

Steps

  • Segment oranges and remove seeds.
  • Add all ingredients to blender.
  • Blend until smooth and luminous.
  • Taste and tweak sweetness.

Approximate nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: ~180
  • Vitamin C: high
  • Protein: ~8g
  • Sugars: natural fruit + honey
