📚 Key Art Concepts

  • Contrast: The difference between elements to create visual interest.
  • Balance: Distribution of visual weight across a composition.
  • Hierarchy: Guiding the viewer’s eye through importance and scale.
  • Unity: Cohesion among elements to create harmony.
  • Rhythm: Repetition and movement that energize a design.
