LuthorCorp Futures: Smallville Universe Dashboard

This experimental dashboard imagines a future where Lana Lang, Clark Kent, Lex Luthor, and the legacy of LuthorCorp intersect with holidays, birthdates, weather experiments, and art concepts. It is a playful, story-style interface that blends Smallville, Superman, and a Disneyland-inspired castle into one creative space.
