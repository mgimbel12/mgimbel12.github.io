Smallville Law & Order of Families

This charter records the guiding “laws” of the Luthors, Kents, Sullivans, Queens, Currys, and the wider town of Smallville. It is intended as a reference for discussions, essays, and creative projects across all seasons of the series.

Luthors Kents Sullivans Queens Currys Smallville

1. Law & Order of the Luthors

Power is protection. The Luthor code treats law as a tool to be bent, rewritten, or purchased. Control, leverage, and legacy define their sense of order.

2. Law & Order of the Kents

Truth, responsibility, and restraint. The Kents teach that power must serve others and that moral law stands above personal gain.

3. Law & Order of the Sullivans

The public’s right to know. Chloe’s investigative code balances transparency with safety, evidence with empathy.

4. Law & Order of the Queens

Justice beyond the courtroom. The Queens embrace vigilante missions when formal systems fail, holding themselves to a strict internal charter.

5. Law & Order of the Currys

Stewardship of the natural world. AC’s law defends oceans, ecosystems, and those who cannot speak for themselves.

6. Law & Order of Smallville

Community, memory, and survival. The town’s unwritten rules blend local authority, shared trauma, and resilience.

7. Law & Orders of Families & Friends

The true order of Smallville emerges where these codes collide: Kent vs. Luthor, secrecy vs. truth, institutions vs. vigilantes. Each character carries a personal law that shapes the town’s future.
