When colors feel too dark
Feelings & signs

Writing or drawing your feelings is like choosing a crayon—once you see the color, it’s easier to work with it.

Little colors that help
Calming actions
Color one page slowly Slow belly breaths (in 4, out 4) Hold a favorite toy or pillow Ask a grown‑up to sit with me

You don’t have to fix the whole picture—just add one gentle color at a time.

Safe places on my map
Comfort zones

A safe place can be a room, a chair, a corner, or anywhere you feel more okay being yourself.

My safety color crew
Trusted people

Let these people know they are on your “safety color crew” so they understand how important they are.

Immediate safety
