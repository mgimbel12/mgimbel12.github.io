🍓 Strawberries & Creme “Crepe” Bowl
Strawberries & Creme Creep (Crepe‑style bowl)
Light & creamy

A crepe‑inspired dessert without the frying pan: soft “crepe” pieces, strawberries, and a creamy, lighter topping in a bowl.

You’ll need:

  • •Strawberries, sliced
  • •Thin vanilla or plain tortillas / very thin pancakes (crepe substitute)
  • •Fat‑free Greek yogurt or fat‑free whipped topping
  • •Vanilla extract and a touch of sweetener (stevia, honey, or sugar)
  • •Optional: a sprinkle of cinnamon or cocoa powder

How to assemble:

  • 1.Warm the thin tortilla or crepe lightly, then cut into strips or small squares.
  • 2.Mix fat‑free yogurt with vanilla and sweetener to taste.
  • 3.Layer “crepe” pieces, strawberries, and spoonfuls of creme in a bowl.
  • 4.Finish with a dusting of cinnamon or cocoa for a café‑style look.
🫐 Strawberry & Blueberry Fat‑Free Parfait
Layered Berry Cloud Parfait
Fat‑free dessert

A tall glass of berries and airy cream—pretty enough for guests, light enough for everyday.

You’ll need:

  • •Strawberries, sliced
  • •Blueberries (fresh or thawed frozen)
  • •Fat‑free vanilla yogurt or fat‑free whipped topping
  • •Granola or crushed whole‑grain cereal (optional crunch)
  • •A drizzle of honey or sugar‑free syrup (optional)

How to assemble:

  • 1.In a clear glass, add a spoonful of yogurt or whipped topping.
  • 2.Layer strawberries, then blueberries, then a sprinkle of granola.
  • 3.Repeat layers until the glass is full, ending with berries on top.
  • 4.Drizzle lightly with honey or syrup if you want extra sweetness.
❄️ Frozen Strawberry & Blueberry Bites
Berry‑Cream Freezer Bites
Make‑ahead

Little frozen clusters of berries and fat‑free cream—perfect for hot days or movie nights.

You’ll need:

  • •Strawberries, chopped small
  • •Blueberries
  • •Fat‑free yogurt (vanilla or plain)
  • •Mini muffin tin or silicone molds
  • •Optional: a tiny bit of sweetener

How to assemble:

  • 1.Mix chopped strawberries and blueberries with yogurt in a bowl.
  • 2.Spoon the mixture into mini muffin cups or silicone molds.
  • 3.Freeze for at least 2–3 hours until solid.
  • 4.Pop out and serve as cold, creamy berry bites.
Tip: Using fat‑free yogurt or whipped topping keeps the dessert lighter, but you can mix in a little regular cream if you want a richer texture for guests.
