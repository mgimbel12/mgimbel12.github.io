Clean ingredient stories
Face & makeup cleanse
Gentle skin focus
Imagine a line that leans toward:
  • Plant‑based cleansers instead of harsh sulfates.
  • Fragrance‑light or fragrance‑free options for sensitive skin.
  • Simple ingredient lists you can actually read.
Always check real product labels and talk to professionals if you have skin concerns.
Nails, hands & care
Nourishing touch
Concept ideas for kinder nail and hand care:
  • Conditioning oils (like jojoba or sweet almond) instead of only drying solvents.
  • Formulas that avoid very strong, irritating solvents where possible.
  • Moisturizing hand creams after polish removal.
This is general information, not medical advice or a guarantee of “healing.”
Home & environment
Cleaning with care
For home cleaning, some people look for:
  • Biodegradable, plant‑based surfactants.
  • Avoiding unnecessary dyes or heavy synthetic fragrances.
  • Clear disposal and recycling guidance on packaging.
Always follow safety labels and local guidelines for any cleaning product.
