Mental Health Hotlines

National Mental Health Hotline: 866-903-3787
Free & confidential 24/7 support for anxiety, depression, PTSD, and more.
Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: 988
Call or text 988 for immediate help. Available nationwide, 24/7.
Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
Text-based support for any crisis, available 24/7.
The Trevor Project (LGBTQ+): 1-866-488-7386
Support for LGBTQ+ youth in crisis. Confidential and compassionate.
Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-7233
Help for those experiencing abuse or unsafe relationships.
